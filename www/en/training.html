Training

Whether to refresh existing knowledge or to shift gears and move to a new technology, trainings are an effective way to make sure that everyone is rowing in the same direction. Adding features is really only a small part of software engineering, the way bigger part is long-term maintenance. Natural fluctuation and restructuring can severely slow down projects if knowledge was distributed unequally. By focusing on building a solid foundation, we want to strengthen Your ability to adapt, and show that "agile" isn't just a marketing term.

Developer Essentials

We believe that a short and carefully curated list of technologies beats a long list of specialized tools almost every time. If used properly, these technologies provide a level of interoperability that quickly offsets any lack of specialization. Fewer technologies also enable quicker onboarding, prevent knowledge silos and foster a mindset of "Do we really need this?". Our goal is to provide You with the foundation to be maximally effective using minimal resources. All listed technologies are free and open-source, and we heavily use them ourselves, e.g. for this website.
