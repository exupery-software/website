Engineering

Architecture

  • How can I separate my business logic from other layers like controllers and storage do increaase reusability?
  • What are my dependencies and which ones do I really need? How do I vet dependencies and keep them up-to-date?
  • How can I model my (relational) data to be consistent and stay flexible for future changes?

Coding

  • How can I create regular analyses and reports from my data and use them to easily take further decisions?
  • What are some ways to connect different and independent systems through a central service?
  • How can I leverage my version control system and code reviews to increase the quality of my code? What role does a single commit play in the overall maintainability of my code base?

Testing

  • What can I do to make my code more testable?
  • How can I substitute, i.e. "mock", other types and external systems in my tests?
  • Which type of test, e.g. unit, integration, regression, should I even write and why?
