Consulting

Technology

  • What is my current tech stack? Is it still up-to-date and contributing to my sustainability?
  • Which parts increase my productivity? Which ones are redundant or slowing me down?
  • How can I better leverage synergies within my tech stack?

Processes

  • How are software creation and maintenance linked to each other and what can I do to prevent technical debt?
  • What are the blockers and bottlenecks in my processes and how can I effectively remove them?
  • How can I improve my feedback loops, decrease cycle time and establish a meaningful definition of "done"?

Recruiting

  • What traits should I be looking for in a candidate?
  • Which environment attracts and retains talents?
  • How are my tech stack and processes contributing?
